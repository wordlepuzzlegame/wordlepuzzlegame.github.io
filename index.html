Pro Strategies

Expert Tips for the Daily Wordle Puzzle

Level up your wordle puzzle game with these strategic tips used by top players around the world.

1

Start with Vowel-Rich Words

Open your wordle word puzzle with words containing multiple vowels like AUDIO, RAISE, STARE, or CRANE. Covering 2–3 vowels in your first guess dramatically narrows the solution space.

2

Maximize Letter Coverage Early

Use your first two guesses to test as many distinct letters as possible. Avoid reusing letters from a previous guess in the same position where they showed gray.

3

Think in Word Patterns

English words follow predictable patterns. Common endings like -TION, -IGHT, -OULD and common beginnings like ST-, TR-, CH- can help you fill in missing letters faster in the daily wordle puzzle.

4

Use Yellow Letters Wisely

Yellow means the letter exists in the word — just not there. Move yellow letters to different positions in your next guess. Don't ignore them, and don't guess them in the same spot again.

5

Eliminate Common Traps

Beware of doubled letters (like LLAMA or TEETH) — they can trip up your deductions. Pay attention to whether a letter appears once or might repeat in the free wordle puzzle online solution.

6

Build a Consistent Opener

Many wordle puzzle pros use the same opening word every day — it removes decision fatigue and helps you track improvement. Popular starters: SLATE, CRANE, TRACE, STARE, or ADIEU.
