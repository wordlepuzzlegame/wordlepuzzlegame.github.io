Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2024

At WordlePuzzle, accessible from wordlepuzzle.github.io, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by WordlePuzzle and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

WordlePuzzle does not require a user account or registration. We do not collect personal identification information like your name or email address unless you contact us directly. We may collect non-personal data such as browser type and game statistics (stored locally on your device).

2. Log Files

WordlePuzzle follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamps, and referring/exit pages.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, WordlePuzzle uses "cookies". These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences and game progress (e.g., your current streak or completed puzzles).

4. Third-Party Privacy Policies

Our Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We may use third-party services like Google Analytics to understand site traffic. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party servers for more detailed information.

5. Children's Information

WordlePuzzle is an educational and fun game suitable for everyone. We do not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13.

6. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
